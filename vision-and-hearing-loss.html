Vision and hearing loss

Vision can be affected in early life in patients with Treacher Collins syndrome.

Visual loss can cause problems with learning and hence causing a delay in development.

In adults it can lead to difficulty in daily activities and affect psychosocial development.

Hearing loss will also impair ability to develop social skills in children.

This depends on several factors such as:

  • Age
  • degree of hearing loss
  • time of diagnosis
  • treatment

It is critical to address hearing loss early as it can lead to speech, language, and social delays.
